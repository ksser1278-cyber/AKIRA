# User Prompt for GPT: "The Syntactic DNA & Quantitative Audit"

**Role:** You are a Senior Computational Linguist and Japanese Songwriting Analyst. Your goal is to provide **Quantitative and Syntactic Blueprints** for the writing styles of **PinocchioP** and **DECO*27** to break a "Repetitive AI Template" problem.

---

## Part 1: PinocchioP - "Syntactic Meta-Irony"

1. **Sentence Endings (Gobi) Distribution:** Analyze the frequency and impact of specific 어미(Sentence endings) like `〜って`, `〜だろ`, `〜だよな`, `〜なんだが`. How do these create the "Irony Gap"?
2. **Mora Density Mapping:** Calculate the average Mora (Syllable) count per line in a typical PinocchioP Verse vs. Chorus. 
    - Provide a **"Syllabic Sabotage Rule"** (e.g., "Violate the 7-5 rhythm at line 3 of every verse to simulate a system glitch").
3. **Noun-to-Verb Ratio:** How does he use "Information-Heavy Nouns" to weigh down his verses? Provide a list of 10 "Abstract-to-Banal" linguistic pairs (e.g., 'Eternity' -> 'Discount Sticker').

---

## Part 2: DECO*27 - "Somatic Phoneticism"

1. **Phonetic Texture Audit:** Analyze the use of **Plosives (P, T, K, D)** vs. **Fricatives (S, H)** in DECO*27's hooks (e.g., 'Vampire', 'Rabbit Hole'). 
    - Why do his lyrics feel "Hard" and "Percussive"? Provide a **"Phonetic Blueprint"** for a new chorus.
2. **Pronoun Geometry:** MAP the frequency of `僕 (Boku)` and `君 (Kimi)`. 
    - How many lines on average pass before a direct address occurs? 
    - Suggest a **"Pronoun Sabotage Pattern"** to break the standard 1-to-1 dialogue loop.
3. **Sensing Chains (Te-form):** Analyze the use of `〜て, 〜て, 〜て` chains. How many consecutive "Te-form" verbs does he use to simulate breathlessness? Provide a rule for "Controlled Hyperventilation" in lyrics.

## Part 3: Evaluation Criteria for AI
Provide a **"Diversity Scoring Metric"** (1-10) to judge if a generated lyric is:
