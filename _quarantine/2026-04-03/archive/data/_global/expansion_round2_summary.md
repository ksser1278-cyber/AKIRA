# Expansion Round 2 Summary

## Highest Value Producers for New Pipeline
- **Kanaria & Kairiki Bear**: Both artists have extremely distinct, tight styles that currently suffer from low data diversity in the pipeline. Adding tracks like *Yoidore Shirazu* (Kanaria) and *Darling Dance* (Kairiki Bear) will broaden their generation bounds beyond literal clones of their most famous anchor tracks.
- **Iyowa & Syudou**: Adding new support producers is critical at this stage to prevent complete overfitting to the Voca-Rock scene and the high-BPM trance scene. 
  - **Iyowa** fills a massive gap in the "dizzy, off-kilter, jazz-chord" harmonic space.
  - **Syudou** provides slower, cynical, swing/hop rhythms that prevent all irony in the engine from defaulting to PinocchioP's lightning-fast BPM.

## Most Underrepresented Engine Mode
- **dark_cute_breakdown**: Currently, this mode is heavily reliant on DECO*27's "Vampire" archetype and risks becoming a pure "yandere romance" generator. Expanding this mode with Kairiki Bear's glitch-panic and Iyowa's dizzy-dread will make the mode far more robust and dimensionally separate from standard pop.

## Top 5 Candidates for Immediate Full Conditioning (Priority Order)
1. **Iyowa - Kyu-kurarin** (Massive style gap filler; introduces soft-panic aesthetic)
2. **Syudou - Bitter Choco Decoration** (Expands `ironic_meta` rhythms dramatically, adds swing/swagger)
3. **Kanaria - Yoidore Shirazu** (Broadens Kanaria away from literal screaming "KING" clones)
4. **DECO*27 - Otome Dissection** (Deepens DECO*27's emotional arc handling beyond pure speed)
5. **Kairiki Bear - Darling Dance** (Establishes the upper limit of the chaotic loop/stutter aesthetic)
