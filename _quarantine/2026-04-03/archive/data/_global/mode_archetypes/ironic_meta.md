# Core Definition
Ironic Meta captures songs that break the fourth wall, critique their own existence, or observe society with extreme, detachment-fueled irony. It relies on a high-energy exterior hiding a deeply cynical or exhausted interior.
**Representative Producers**: PinocchioP, MARETU, Kanaria.

# Title Types
- **Suitable**: Meta-commentary, cynical buzzwords, blunt declarative facts, sarcastic expressions of joy.
- **Unsuitable**: Sincere emotional pleas, pure romantic phrases, overly poetic metaphors.

# Hook Logic
- **Repetition Pressure**: High. The hook acts as an inescapable, mindless slogan.
- **Title Drop Timing**: Immediate or perfectly synced as a punchline to the pre-chorus setup.
- **Hook Nucleus Length**: Must be punchy. Often 2-4 words that can be chanted.
- **Rule**: The hook must sound catchy but emotionally dead, detached, or manic.

# Section Flow
- **Verse**: Sets the scene or establishes the rule of the absurd world. Rapid observation of flaws.
- **Pre-Chorus**: The realization of exhaustion, the breaking of the facade, or the punchline setup.
- **Chorus**: Manic acceptance of the absurdity. Heavy chanting, high-BPM synth/rock release.
- **Bridge**: The mask slips. A brief moment of terrifying sincerity or eerie acoustic quiet.
- **Final Chorus**: The mask is slammed back on, but worn more desperately. Tempo feels heavier.

# Preferred Imagery Classes
- Plastic, screens, digital noise, fake internet smiles
- Commercial products, branding, supermarkets
- Masks, avatars, anonymity, faceless crowds
- Broken societal rules, hypocritical laws

# Release Pattern
- **Ironic Reveal**: The tension does not drop into a traditional euphoric pop release; it drops into a sonic punchline, a cynical sigh, or manic, forced laughter. The "victory" is surviving the absurdity.

# Critic Failure Cases
- **Flat Escalation**: Building up to a generic pop drop without a narrative punchline or ironic twist.
- **False Sincerity**: The lyrics accidentally become genuinely inspiring or heartbreaking, breaking the irony shield entirely rather than just slipping the mask briefly.
- **Weak Title-Binding**: The title is just thrown in, rather than functioning as the core sarcastic slogan of the song.

# Leakage Risk
Overfitting to PinocchioP's specific "rapid-fire mundane listing" worldview. The mode must also support Kanaria's arrogant meta-superiority and MARETU's hostile, polite-keigo meta-threats.

# Safe Originality Zone
Critiques of modern digital life, parasocial relationships, hyper-consumerism, or the nature of creating art itself, wrapped in a hyper-upbeat, danceable structure that drastically belies the cynical message. Do not write a sad ballad; write a desperate party track.
