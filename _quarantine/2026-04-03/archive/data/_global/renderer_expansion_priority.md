# Renderer Expansion Priority Brief

This brief summarizes the current renderer gaps for core artists and the smallest useful additions for the next expansion pass.

---

## 1. Priority Tier 1

### A. Kanaria (Priority: Highest)

- **Current gap:** Dedicated title and hook phrase shaping is limited.
- **Why it matters:** Chorus openings can drift toward generic fallback.
- **Minimal next addition:** Add artist-specific chorus opening templates and title-binding templates.

### B. Maretu (Priority: Highest)

- **Current gap:** Explicit polite-cruel phrasing support is limited.
- **Why it matters:** Artist voice can collapse into generic dark phrasing.
- **Minimal next addition:** Add constrained phrase templates for polite accusation and cold judgment.

---

## 2. Priority Tier 2

### A. Kairiki Bear (Priority: High)

- **Current gap:** Stable stutter handling is not verified.
- **Why it matters:** Repetition-heavy hooks can lose phrase stability.
- **Minimal next addition:** Add phrase templates that preserve repeated openings without breaking the mora grid.

### B. Iyowa (Priority: High)

- **Current gap:** Renderer-specific imagery shaping is limited.
- **Why it matters:** Imagery can become generic or lose contrast across sections.
- **Minimal next addition:** Add constrained imagery templates for concrete-surreal transitions.

---

## 3. Priority Tier 3

### A. Syudou (Priority: Medium)

- **Current gap:** Diagnostic interjection shaping is limited.
- **Why it matters:** Tone can default to generic aggressive phrasing.
- **Minimal next addition:** Add constrained interjection templates for short diagnostic lines.

### B. Neru (Priority: Medium)

- **Current gap:** Explicit industrial-surgical phrase shaping is limited.
- **Why it matters:** Surface phrasing can drift into generic rock phrasing.
- **Minimal next addition:** Add constrained imperative and object templates for industrial imagery.

---

## 4. Evaluation Criteria

1. **Generic Fallback Risk:** Highest priority goes to artists whose voice is most easily lost in the default surface.
2. **Phrase Stability Risk:** Priority goes to artists whose hooks or repeated phrases are unstable under current rendering.
3. **Data Readiness:** Only artists with canonical JSON are considered for immediate expansion.
