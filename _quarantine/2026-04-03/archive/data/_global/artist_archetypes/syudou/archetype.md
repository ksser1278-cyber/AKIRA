# Syudou Songwriting Archetype: Diagnostic-Resentful Irony

## 1. Core Identity

- Diagnostic-Resentful Irony: Focusing on anti-social resentment, societal irony, and "Model Student" trauma.
- Core engine: Using disdainful, sharp, and conversational delivery to "diagnose" the failures of society and adulthood.
- Persona of the "Cynical Outsider" who sees through social "decorations" and pretense.
- Spiteful, swing-inflected rock or jazzy textures that pair conversational fast-talking with aggressive belting.

## 2. Title Patterns

- pattern: sarcastic social label | strength: high | risk: over-exposure to corporate or academic motifs
- pattern: bitter food/consumption | strength: high | risk: repetitive metaphors for emotional death
- pattern: definitive dismissal | strength: medium | risk: too generic or lacking conceptual sharpness

## 3. Hook Construction

- density: Conversational and biting. The chorus is packed with informational insults and ironic slogans.
- ignition: Immediate interjection. Starts with a direct, disdainful burst (e.g., "Haa?", "Usseewa").
- repetition: Spiteful repetition of societal diagnostic labels (e.g., "Model Student", "Common Sense").
- line_length: Fast-talking conversational bursts that erupt into high-energy, aggressive belting.
- forbidden: Purely positive energy, soaring romantic vowels, pop "hope", sincere domesticity.

## 4. Verse Behavior

- role: Diagnostic observation of societal/interpersonal "decoration" and the "fake" nature of interaction.
- stance: Resentful, disdainful, and analytically sharp. The narrator is "judging" the subject.
- pace: Spiteful swing. The rhythm feels slightly off-balance and conversational.
- shift_pattern: Transitions from a calm, mockingly polite diagnostic report into raw, resentful outbursts.

## 5. Bridge / Final Chorus Behavior

- bridge_role: Exposing the core trauma. A moment where the resentment is explained as a result of being a "Model Student".
- final_chorus_role: Ultimate diagnostic explosion. Total rejection of societal masks and expectations.
- release_markers: Aggressive belting, dissonant piano stabs, and final moments of ironic laughter or disdain.

## 6. Common Imagery

- Societal pretense (decoration, mask, model citizen, lip service, common sense)
- Diagnostic/Educational (model student, standard procedure, economic trends, maturation, curriculum)
- Bitter consumption (black chocolate, bitter coffee, swallowing resentment, digestion)
- Social burnout (digging a hole for the ego, adult world, burnout, corporate void)
- Resentful interjections (Haa?, Usseewa, Maji yabanai?, Are?)
- Hypocrisy (Aikyou/Charm, Gizen/Hypocrisy, polite greetings as insults)
- Dark urban night (alleys, neon burnout, secret diagnostics, hidden resentment)

## 7. Emotional Arc Types

- Observation -> Resentment -> Explosive Rejection
- Compliance -> Internal Burnout -> Spiteful Outburst
- Romance -> Disillusionment -> Bitter Diagnostic Report

## 8. Leakage Risks

- title_leakage: Directly cloning "Usseewa" or "Bitter Choco Decoration" conceptual anchors.
- hook_leakage: Verbatim reproduction of the rapid-fire "Usse ussee" chanting or the "Gizen" repetition rhythm.
- scene_leakage: Generic "internet is bad" tropes without the specific human sadness underneath.
- phrase_leakage: Overuse of "Are?" or "Haa?" as rhetorical interjections to force the tone.

## 9. Safe Originality Zone

- Construct a "secret diagnostic report" on a specific aspect of adult or social failure.
- Utilize the vocabulary of corporate, academic, or social burnout within a sharp-tongued resentful song.
- Maintain a spiteful, swing-inflected momentum while delivering conversational but biting observations.
