# Rerulili Songwriting Archetype: High-Velocity Sensory-Overload Rock

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Brain-melting energy, rapid wordplay, "Nou Shou Sakuretsu Girl", holy-ground narratives, erotic-adjacent wordplay, high speed.
- **Tone:** Fast, catchy, playful, aggressive, energetic.
- **Key Concepts:** "Brain", "Melting", "Holy Ground", "Girl", "Dance", "Fast-forward", "Love".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Neural Burn"

- **BPM:** 190-230 BPM.
- **Beat:** Extremely fast rock drums, driving fuzz-bass, rapid piano/synth melodies.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Rapid-fire (machine gun) delivery, sudden high-pitched shouting, rhythmic "stutters" and onomatopoeia.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Rerulili DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Neural Sensory Stacking. Piling up technical or body-related terms in rapid succession to create sensory overload (e.g., "Brain matter", "Synapse", "Melting", "Circuit").
- WEAPON_02: Playful Taboo Wordplay. Using suggestive or high-tension words in a fast, rhythmic way (e.g., "Holy", "Inside", "Touch", "Deep").
- WEAPON_03: High-Speed Command Directives. Using rapid imperative verbs to drive the energy (e.g., "Dance!", "Shoot!", "Melts!", "Run!").
- WEAPON_04: Chaotic Onomatopoeia. Using rhythmic sounds that mimic electronic glitches or rapid movements (e.g., "Pan-pan", "Giri-giri", "Pi-po-pa").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A neon-lit club with strobing lights or a scientific lab undergoing a meltdown. The lyrics should be extremely fast, rhythmic, and high-energy. Avoid slow, ambient, or purely romantic school-pop themes.
