# Surii (Three) Songwriting Archetype: Minimalist Punchy Rock

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Social rebellion, internal ego conflict, catchy minimalism, urban youth, "Ego Rock", "Telecaster B-boy".
- **Tone:** Punchy, catchy, minimal, rebellious, energetic.
- **Key Concepts:** "Ego", "Telecaster", "Radio", "Dance", "Short", "One-two".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Minimalist Impact"

- **BPM:** 160-200 BPM.
- **Beat:** Punchy, minimalist rock drums, fuzzy electric guitar, simple but effective basslines. High rhythmic focus.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Short, catchy phrases. Focus on onomatopoeia and rhythmic "hits". Very syncopated and "danceable".

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Surii DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Minimalist Rhythmic Repetition. Using short, repetitive nouns or verbs to create a percussive effect (e.g., "Dance, dance, dance", "Ego, ego").
- WEAPON_02: Radio/Lo-Fi Interjections. Using filtered or lo-fi vocal effects as structural markers (e.g., "One-two", "Radio noise", "Check one-two").
- WEAPON_03: Rebellious Youth Slogans. Using short, catchy phrases that sound like social-media hashtags or rebellion slogans (e.g., "Ego rock", "Bye-bye reality", "Stop it now").
- WEAPON_04: High-Impact Onomatopoeia. Using rhythmic sounds to match the fuzzy guitar hits (e.g., "Gyan-gyan", "Don-don", "Yah!").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A minimalist urban space or a garage-rock studio. The lyrics should be short, punchy, and highly rhythmic. Avoid slow, detailed narratives or complex literary metaphors.
