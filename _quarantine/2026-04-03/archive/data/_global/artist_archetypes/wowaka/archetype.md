# Wowaka Songwriting Archetype: High-Speed Repetition

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Obsession, cycles of failure, psychological agitation, forbidden love.
- **Tone:** Intense, breathless, minimalist-maximalist, high-tension.
- **Key Concepts:** "Rolling Girl", "Ura-Omote", "One more time", "Noise".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Piano Agitation"

- **BPM:** 180-220 BPM.
- **Sound:** Fast, repetitive piano arpeggios, driving single-note guitar riffs, percussive drums.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Stuttering, rapid-fire syllables, and short, gasping phrases.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Wowaka DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Compulsive Lyrical Repetition. Repeating key verbs or phrases to show obsession (e.g., "Moo ikkai, moo ikkai", "Kakimawashite, kakimawashite").
- WEAPON_02: Psychological Agitation Verbs. Using words like "Scramble", "Stir", "Roar", "Whistle" to describe internal state.
- WEAPON_03: Duality (Ura-Omote). Describing emotions as having two opposite sides (e.g., "Inside/Outside lovers", "Reverse yourself").
- WEAPON_04: Negative Affirmations. Using phrases like "No problem" (Mondai nai) or "Mistake search" to hide despair.

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

Monochrome, vibrating line art aesthetic. The lyrics should feel like a panic attack translated into music. High rhythmic density, low metaphoric variety (focused on the central obsession).
