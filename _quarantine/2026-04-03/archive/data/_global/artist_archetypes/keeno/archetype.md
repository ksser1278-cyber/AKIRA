# Keeno Songwriting Archetype: Ambient Post-Rock / Ballad

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Rainy days, ambient loneliness, glowing lights in the dark, fading memories, breathy melancholy, "Glow".
- **Tone:** Soft, ambient, heartbreaking, slow, emotionally translucent.
- **Key Concepts:** "Glow", "Dust", "Crack", "Rain", "Night", "Memory", "Fading".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Fading Echo"

- **BPM:** 70-100 BPM.
- **Beat:** Slow, ambient rock drums (often with a rimshot focus), atmospheric reverb-heavy guitars, subtle piano.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Breathy, slow, long sustained high notes with a "crying" quality. Highly expressive and fragile delivery.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Keeno DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Atmospheric Weather Melancholy. Using rain or ambient light to set a mood of fading grief (e.g., "Glow in the rain", "Wet morning", "Distant streetlights").
- WEAPON_02: Fragile Physical Decay Metaphors. Describing emotions through cracking, fading, or dusty objects (e.g., "Cracking heart", "Dusty memory", "Fading color").
- WEAPON_03: Breathy Half-Phrases. Using short, whispered or breathy interjections that sound like a sigh (e.g., "Ah...", "Wait...", "Don't go").
- WEAPON_04: Nocturnal Urban Stillness. Describing the city as a quiet, empty place where one is alone (e.g., "Empty room", "Night air", "Quiet breathing").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A bedroom during a heavy rainstorm at night or a quiet park under a dim streetlight. The lyrics should be slow, ambient, and deeply emotional. Avoid fast, loud, or upbeat pop-rock cliches.
