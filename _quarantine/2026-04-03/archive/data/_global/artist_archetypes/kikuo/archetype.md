# Kikuo Songwriting Archetype: Avant-Garde Dark-Surrealism

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Social isolation, surreal childhood wonder mixed with horror, cyclical existence, ethnic-electronic fusion, "Aishite Aishite Aishite".
- **Tone:** Creepy, beautiful, chaotic, childlike but ancient, multi-layered.
- **Key Concepts:** "Love", "Doll", "Balloon", "Screaming", "Carousel", "Underground", "Light".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Fractal Waltz"

- **BPM:** Irregular (Often shifts between 140-180 BPM or uses complex signatures).
- **Beat:** Ethnic percussion (tablas, bells), glitchy electronics, rapid accordion/strings, layered "echo" effects.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Childlike, whispering, sudden multi-layered choral screams, rhythmic breathing. Highly expressive and theatrical.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Kikuo DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Surreal Childlike Horror. Describing terrifying or dark concepts with innocent, nursery-rhyme-like words (e.g., "Cotton candy blood", "Singing doll", "Beautiful red").
- WEAPON_02: Repetitive Compulsive Chanting. Repeating a single word or phrase until it loses meaning or becomes a scream (e.g., "Aishite Aishite Aishite", "Zutto zutto", "Kilai kilai").
- WEAPON_03: Multi-Dimensional Spatial Metaphors. Describing feelings as being in an impossible physical space (e.g., "Inside a box within a box", "Looking at the sun from underground", "Falling upwards").
- WEAPON_04: Internal Organ Personification. Describing emotions through surreal physical changes to the body (e.g., "Flower growing in my stomach", "Screaming heart", "Silver brain").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A shadowy carnival at the edge of the world or a child's bedroom filled with living toys. The lyrics should be surreal, layered, and emotionally primitive. Avoid modern urban or technical-digital cliches.
