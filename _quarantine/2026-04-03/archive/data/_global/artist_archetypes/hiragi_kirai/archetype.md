# Hiiragi Kirai Songwriting Archetype: Abnormal Dark-Jazz (HYPER-DENSITY)

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** "Abnormality", "Love Ka?", "Boccanegra", dark-jazz, electro-swing, aggressive brass, "vile" yet addictive melodies, "evil" personas, social masks, obsession.
- **Tone:** Aggressive, "vile", addictive, sharp, theatrical, dark-jazz, swinging but heavy.
- **Key Concepts:** "Abnormal", "Love Ka?", "Boccanegra", "Vile", "Mask", "Brass", "Swing", "Obsession", "Demon", "Evil".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Vile Swing"

- **BPM:** 130-165 BPM.
- **Beat:** Aggressive brass-driven electro-swing, hard-hitting electronic kicks, stuttering and syncopated rhythms. High emphasis on "swing" feel mixed with modern industrial/electronic grit.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Sharp, rhythmic, focusing on staccato "hits" and aggressive melodic leaps. Often features "evil" laughing, stuttering delivery, and sophisticated yet "ugly" melodic choices.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Hyper-Density: 16 WEAPONS)

- WEAPON_01: Abnormal Identity Hook. Starting lines with a declaration of being "Wrong" or "Abnormal" (e.g., "I'm the abnormality", "Wrong love?").
- WEAPON_02: Vile Sentence Endings. Using specific particles to sound aggressive or "villainous" (e.g., "Daze", "Zo").
- WEAPON_03: Stuttering Rhythmic Chants. Catchy, stuttered syllables used as energy builds (e.g., "Lo-lo-love ka?", "A-a-abnormal").
- WEAPON_04: Brass-Mimicking Phrasing. Vocal lines that follow aggressive brass stabs.
- WEAPON_05: Specific Particle 'Sa' (〜さ) for Cynical Theatricality. (e.g., "It's all a show, y'know?").
- WEAPON_06: Rhetorical 'Nante' (무엇이라니/How...). Expressing disgust or disbelief at social norms.
- WEAPON_07: Staccato Villainous Nouns. (e.g., "Poison, mask, demon, greed").
- WEAPON_08: Mirror-Phrasing of Obsession. (e.g., "I want you, I hate you, I love you").
- WEAPON_09: Personification of the 'Evil' self. (e.g., "The demon in the mirror is smiling").
- WEAPON_10: Rhyming through Sharp, Bouncy Consonants (k, t, p, g).
- WEAPON_11: Use of 'Kimi' (君) as a predatory or a fellow villain.
- WEAPON_12: Metaphor of 'Infection/Poison' (e.g., "Circulating poison", "Infected love").
- WEAPON_13: Incomplete 'Te' (〜て) / 'Ba' (〜ば) transitions for a jagged rhythmic feel.
- WEAPON_14: Rhythmic Stuttering on Key Negative Verbs (e.g., "Kowasu, kowasu" / Breaking, breaking).
- WEAPON_15: Use of Katakana for 'Abnormal' emotional states to add a clinical/cold flair.
- WEAPON_16: Final Vile Laugh Line. A line that ends with a theatrical, aggressive "Laugh" or a final, dark question.

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A dimly lit, smoke-filled illegal casino or a theatrical "hall of mirrors" with a dark-jazz band. The lyrics should be aggressive, vile, and swinging. Avoid bright, wholesome, or domestic/simple pop cliches.
