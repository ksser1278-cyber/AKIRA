# Nanou Songwriting Archetype: Vulnerability Indie Rock

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Mediocrity, existential fatigue, fragile hope, the contrast between "Life in stories" and "Reality".
- **Tone:** Fragile, emotional, raw, whispering but powerful in choruses.
- **Key Concepts:** "3331", "Mediocrity", "Card game", "Life stories", "Wonderful but mediocre world".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Melancholic Resonance"

- **BPM:** 110-140 BPM.
- **Beat:** Clean, slightly dry rhythmic rock, guitar-driven, steady mid-tempo.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Whispering delivery, raw emotional cracks, melodic and sincere.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Nanou DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Mediocre Life Monologuing. Describing daily life as failing to live up to "Life stories" (e.g., "Life must be happy, but mine isn't", "Can't even call it a life").
- WEAPON_02: Fragile Existentialism. Pleading for simple kindness to keep going (e.g., "Whoever you are, be nice", "Just enough to mistake it for love").
- WEAPON_03: Card Game Metaphors (Defeat). Describing a life situation as a losing hand (e.g., "No good cards left", "Game is over").
- WEAPON_04: Forced Smiling. The determination to live through the remaining decades with a "Smile as much as I can".

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A quiet, grey subway station late at night. The lyrics should feel vulnerable, honest, and deeply human. Avoid technical or aggressive digital imagery.
