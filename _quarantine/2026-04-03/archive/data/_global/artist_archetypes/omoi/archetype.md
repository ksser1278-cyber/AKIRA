# Omoi Songwriting Archetype: Melodic Anthemic Rock

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Explosive energy, "Teo", high-velocity rock, anthemic choruses, unstoppable youth, bright digital noise.
- **Tone:** Powerful, bright, high-energy, uplifting but intense.
- **Key Concepts:** "Teo", "Hand", "Light", "Full speed", "Vibration", "Green", "Melody".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Explosive Anthem"

- **BPM:** 190-220 BPM.
- **Beat:** Driving rock drums with heavy crash-cymbal focus, very melodic bass, bright synthesized rock guitars.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** High-register, powerful delivery. Long sustained notes over rapid drum sections. Focus on "reaching" for the melody.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Omoi DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Kinetic Power Directives. Using energy-based verbs to describe emotional shifts (e.g., "Full speed", "Vibrate", "Explode", "Transmit").
- WEAPON_02: Anthemic Reaching Metaphors. Describing the act of singing or feeling as "reaching" for a light or a hand (e.g., "Take my hand", "Reaching for the stars", "Stretching the voice").
- WEAPON_03: Sensory Vibration Terms. Focusing on the physical sensation of music on the body (e.g., "Echo in the chest", "Tingling", "Resonant soul").
- WEAPON_04: Rapid Burst Affirmations. Short, intense bursts of positivity or determination (e.g., "Let's go!", "One more time!", "Right now!").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A massive open stadium under a bright digital sky. The lyrics should be high-energy, melodic, and anthemic. Avoid slow, dark, or overly abstract literary themes.
