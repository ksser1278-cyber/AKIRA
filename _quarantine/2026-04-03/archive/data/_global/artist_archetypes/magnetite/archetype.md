# Hiragi Magnetite Songwriting Archetype: Technical Dark Alternative

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Ambiguity of existence, ghost imagery, structural complexity, internal resonance.
- **Tone:** Technical, edgy, mysterious, powerful.
- **Key Concepts:** "Ghost", "Layer", "Mirror", "Alternative energy", "Technical reef".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Symphonic Chaos"

- **BPM:** 150-180 BPM.
- **Beat:** Complex technical guitar riffs, alternative rock groove, electronic structural layers.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Precise, energetic (Flower-vocaloid style), syncopated structural delivery.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Magnetite DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Technical Ghost Metaphors. Describing living beings as "ghosts" or ambiguous entities (e.g., "Living ghost is alive", "Hiding in the mirror").
- WEAPON_02: Layered Structural Imagery. Focusing on the "layers" (sou) of sound or existence (e.g., "Resonance in the layers", "Shattering layers").
- WEAPON_03: Alternative Assertion. Vocaloid cries for "Proof of living" (Ikiteiru akashi) in a technical world.
- WEAPON_04: Precise Ambiguity. Using technical terms to describe ungraspable feelings.

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A crystalline dark room with shifting geometric shadows. The lyrics should feel "constructed" and structurally high-density. Edgy and technical.
