# Harumaki Gohan Songwriting Archetype: Dreamy Fantasy Realism (HYPER-DENSITY)

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** "Melty Land", stardust, soft-focus nostalgia, blue/white aesthetics, fantasy realism, childhood wonder, gentle melancholy, "Gohan Style" universe.
- **Tone:** Dreamy, soft, nostalgic, gentle, magical, blue-tinted, ethereal.
- **Key Concepts:** "Melty", "Land", "Nightmare", "Stardust", "Blue", "White", "Galaxy", "Snow", "Feather", "Quiet".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Dreamy Drift"

- **BPM:** 120-150 BPM.
- **Beat:** Soft electronic percussion, bright synth pads, gentle piano melodies. High emphasis on "airy" textures and a sense of floating.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Soft, breathant, focusing on high-register vowels and gentle, "melting" melodic lines. Often features rapid but soft delivery that feels like a whisper in a dream.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Hyper-Density: 16 WEAPONS)

- WEAPON_01: Soft-Focus Fantasy Framing. Describing reality as if it's a fading dream or a star (e.g., "Melt into the stardust", "Fading blue planet").
- WEAPON_02: Blue/White Aesthetic Stacking. Focus on colors related to coldness and purity (e.g., "Sapphire sky", "Snowy thoughts", "White paper love").
- WEAPON_03: Gentle Melancholy Hooks. Sad lyrics delivered with a bright, dreamy melody.
- WEAPON_04: Transient Childhood Objects. (e.g., "Old sketchbooks", "Broken toys", "Balloons in the wind").
- WEAPON_05: Specific Particle 'Yo' (〜よ) for Gentle Guidance. (e.g., "Let's go to the stars, shall we?").
- WEAPON_06: Rhetorical 'Sotto' (살며시/Softly). Starting lines with "Quietly..." or "Softly...".
- WEAPON_07: Staccato Ethereal Nouns. (e.g., "Feather, star, tear, cloud").
- WEAPON_08: Mirror-Phrasing of Dreams. (e.g., "Today is a dream, tomorrow is a reality, or is it?").
- WEAPON_09: Personification of the Galaxy. (e.g., "The stars are singing a lullaby").
- WEAPON_10: Rhyming through High, Airy Vowels (i, u).
- WEAPON_11: Use of 'Kimi' (君) as a guiding star or a lost memory.
- WEAPON_12: Metaphor of 'Melting/Dissolving' (e.g., "Identity melting into blue", "Dissolving into the night").
- WEAPON_13: Incomplete 'De' (〜で) / 'Ba' (〜ば) transitions for a floating sensation.
- WEAPON_14: Rhythmic Stuttering on Dreamy Verbs (e.g., "Yureru, yureru" / Swaying, swaying).
- WEAPON_15: Use of Katakana for 'Magical' objects to add a fantasy flair.
- WEAPON_16: Final Fading Miracle Line. A line that describes a small, beautiful miracle before the dream ends.

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A snowy planet at the edge of the galaxy or a quiet garden under a blue moon. The lyrics should be dreamy, soft, and fantasy-focused. Avoid aggressive, dark-industrial, or purely technical cliches.
