# EZFG Songwriting Archetype: Cyber-Minimalist Dark-Pop (HYPER-DENSITY)

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Cyber-minimalism, "Cyber Thunderider", digital precision, rigid rhythms, humanized machines, 0 and 1, technological coldness, "EZFG Style" rhythmic lock.
- **Tone:** Rigid, precise, dark, electronic, cool, mechanical, highly rhythmic.
- **Key Concepts:** "Cyber", "Thunder", "0 and 1", "Binary", "System", "Circuit", "Pulse", "Precision", "Minimal".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Binary Lock"

- **BPM:** 120-150 BPM.
- **Beat:** Hard-hitting electronic percussion, rigid 4/4 kicks, minimal yet sharp synth textures. High focus on absolute rhythmic synchronization and "grid-like" precision.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Rigid, staccato, focusing on absolute pitch-perfection and "mechanical" delivery. Syllables are placed exactly on the beat with minimal emotional vibrato.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Hyper-Density: 16 WEAPONS)

- WEAPON_01: Binary Logic Interjections. Randomly inserting "0" or "1" in context (e.g., "1 of love, 0 of pain").
- WEAPON_02: Rigid Mora-Synchronization. Lines must have identical mora counts in pairs (e.g., 7-7, 8-8).
- WEAPON_03: Technological Coldness Metaphors. (e.g., "Circuit heart", "Silicon tears", "Fiber-optic veins").
- WEAPON_04: Minimalist Directive Language. Using short imperative verbs (e.g., "Load", "Run", "Stop").
- WEAPON_05: Specific Particle 'De' (〜で) for Technical Precision. (e.g., "In this system, then...").
- WEAPON_06: Rhetorical 'Hiteiteki' (부정적/Negative). Using "No" or "False" as a logical state.
- WEAPON_07: Staccato Technical Nouns. (e.g., "Thunder, rider, cyber, light").
- WEAPON_08: Mirror-Image Binary Structure. (e.g., "Left eye 0, right eye 1").
- WEAPON_09: Personification of the Hardware. (e.g., "The fan is screaming").
- WEAPON_10: Rhyming through Hard Consonants (k, t, p).
- WEAPON_11: Use of 'Nani mo nai' (nothing exists) as a digital void motif.
- WEAPON_12: Metaphor of 'Data Transfer' (e.g., "Uploading love", "Packet loss of the soul").
- WEAPON_13: Incomplete 'Te' (〜て) endings followed by a sudden mechanical 'Click'.
- WEAPON_14: Rhythmic Stuttering on Digital Terms (e.g., "Cy-cy-cyber").
- WEAPON_15: Use of Mathematical/Programming operators (+, -, ==, !=) as visual/rhythmic anchors.
- WEAPON_16: Final System-Off Line. A line that ends with "Shut down" or "EOF".

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A server rack in a dark room or a minimalist digital grid. The lyrics should be rigid, technical, and cyber-minimalist. Avoid messy, organic, or overly sentimental/complex human-emotional themes.
