# KurageP Songwriting Archetype: Cynical School Euphoria

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Survival in school hierarchies, exposing secrets, manic joy in social elimination, cynical childhood.
- **Tone:** High-tension, manic, cheerful but cruel, rhythmic.
- **Key Concepts:** "Chururira", "Classroom", "Secret", "Elimination", "Teacher as judge".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Manic School Bell"

- **BPM:** 170-200 BPM.
- **Sound:** Jangle rock guitars, rapid-fire snare hits, whistling, children's SFX used for irony.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** High-pitched, rhythmic, almost like a playground taunt.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (KurageP DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Secret Exposure Interjections. Announcing that a secret is being revealed (e.g., "Saa saa皆様", "Himitsu da yo", "Abashite yarimashou").
- WEAPON_02: School-System Hierarchy. Using school titles or locations as tools of oppression (e.g., "Sensei!", "Classroom", "Empty desk", "Hidden notebook").
- WEAPON_03: Euphoric Elimination Hooks. Catchy, rhythmic chants that celebrate someone leaving or failing (e.g., "Chururira Chururira Daddadda!", "Keshichai mashou").
- WEAPON_04: Fake Politeness. Using overly formal school-speech to deliver insults (e.g., "Hai, mata ashita", "Sayonara, ano ko").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A brightly lit, terrifyingly quiet classroom after hours. The lyrics should feel like a game of social survival. Manic energy hiding a dark core.
