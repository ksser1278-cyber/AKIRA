# Orangestar Songwriting Archetype: High-Altitude Summer Pop

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Endless summer, high-altitude sky, nostalgia for youth, speed of light, melancholic hope.
- **Tone:** Bright but bittersweet, sweeping, high-energy, refreshing.
- **Key Concepts:** "Asu no Yozora", "Staircase", "Cloud", "Summer night", "Circus", "Height".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Summer Gale"

- **BPM:** 180-210 BPM.
- **Beat:** Driving guitar-pop, rapid synth arpeggios, bright and clear piano melodies.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Breathless, high-pitched, sweeping melodic lines with long sustained notes on high vowels (a, e).

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Orangestar DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Atmospheric Expansion. Using sky-related spatial metaphors to describe emotional growth (e.g., "Climbing the sky", "Higher than the clouds", "Endless blue").
- WEAPON_02: Time-Dilation Nostalgic Cries. Describing the moment of youth as both freezing and accelerating (e.g., "Stopping the summer", "Running through the night").
- WEAPON_03: Pure Contrast (Blue vs. Night). Using light and color to contrast isolation and hope (e.g., "Midnight blue", "Dazzling starlight", "Sun-bleached memory").
- WEAPON_04: Conversational Ascent. Dialogue-like phrases asking to go "Somewhere far away" (e.g., "Let's go", "Will you come with me?", "To the other side").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A rooftop overlooking a city in mid-August. The lyrics should feel soaring, wide, and emotionally translucent. Avoid dark, gritty, or electronic-mechanical metaphors.
