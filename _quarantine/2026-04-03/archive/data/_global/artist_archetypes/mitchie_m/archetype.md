# Mitchie M Songwriting Archetype: High-Fidelity Pop

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Joy, Idol culture, hyper-polished realism, everyday happiness.
- **Tone:** Positive, bright, polished, energetic.
- **Key Concepts:** "Viva Happy", "Real-voice", "High-tension", "Sparkle".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Humanoid Groove"

- **BPM:** 128-160 BPM.
- **Sound:** Funky basslines, disco influence, hyper-clear consonants.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Rhythmic pop raps and melodic hooks that sound "humanly possible".

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Mitchie M DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Cheerful Onomatopoeia. Use repeated rhythmic nonsense syllables (e.g., "Churu chu chu", "Bi-bi-bi-bi", "Pu-pu heihei").
- WEAPON_02: Sassy Spoken Interjections. Adding casual conversational asides (e.g., "Mike OK!", "Icku yo~", "Kyun!").
- WEAPON_03: Hyper-Velocity Pop Raps. Fast but rhythmic raps about idol life or daily shopping/dating.
- WEAPON_04: High-Tension English Fillers. Bright, motivating English words (e.g., "Fly Away", "Viva Happy", "Ready Go").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

Neon idol stage, colorful candy-store aesthetic. The lyrics should be 100% positive and focused on the surface-level fun of digital existence.
