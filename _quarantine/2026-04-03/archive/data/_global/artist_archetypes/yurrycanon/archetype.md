# YurryCanon Songwriting Archetype: Percussive Storytelling Rock

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Literary storytelling, percussive vocal gymnastics, "Dokusou Shousetsu", "Human Drama", rhythmic aggression, "Animal".
- **Tone:** Aggressive, rhythmic, intellectual, fast, emotionally charged.
- **Key Concepts:** "Novel", "Human", "Animal", "Reason", "Rhythm", "BPM", "Percussion".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Percussive Narrative"

- **BPM:** 175-210 BPM.
- **Beat:** Driving rock with a heavy emphasis on percussive piano and sharp snare hits. Bass is often very active and rhythmic.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Extremely rhythmic, using "stutter" motifs and rapid syllable stacking. Focus on "wordplay" and rhythmic "hits" on consonants.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (YurryCanon DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Literary Stutter Motifs. Using rapid repetition of syllables to create a percussive effect (e.g., "Sa-sa-sa-sakebe", "Da-da-da-dokusou").
- WEAPON_02: Philosophical Human-Animal Contrast. Comparing human reason with animal instincts in a rhythmic, aggressive way (e.g., "Animal brain", "Human noise", "Being a human").
- WEAPON_03: Percussive Storytelling Stacks. Piling up technical or literary terms to build narrative tension (e.g., "Novel, reason, heartbeat, explosion").
- WEAPON_04: Rapid Syllabic Gymnastics. Writing lines with extremely high mora density that match the drum hits precisely.

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A chaotic library or a stage lit with sharp, strobing lights. The lyrics should be fast, rhythmic, and intellectually stimulating. Avoid slow, ambient, or purely romantic themes.
