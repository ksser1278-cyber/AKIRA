# Sasakure.UK Songwriting Archetype: Chiptune Folk Sci-Fi

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Post-apocalyptic hope, digital loyalty, chiptune mythology, nature reclaiming technology.
- **Tone:** Innocent, bittersweet, digital, folk-infused.
- **Key Concepts:** "Entropy", "Metrology", "＊Hello, Planet.", "Flowerpot", "0 and 1".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Chiptune Odyssey"

- **BPM:** 150-180 BPM.
- **Beat:** Polyrhythmic chiptune arpeggios, complex jazz fusion drumming, glitchy transitions.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Playful yet sophisticated, often using rhythmic displacements and complex melodic leaps.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Sasakure.UK DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Chiptune/Sci-Fi Scientific Metadata. Using technical terms in a poetic, innocent way (e.g., "Entropy of the unknown", "Metrological dreams", "0 and 1 world").
- WEAPON_02: Doomsday "Good Morning". Saying hello to a dying or empty world (e.g., "Ohayougozaimasu", "Hello, Planet", "Good morning to the sunbathing angel").
- WEAPON_03: Botanical Digitalism. Mixing technology with plants (e.g., "Flowerpot with no sprout", "Electronic greenery", "Seed of data").
- WEAPON_04: Recursive Lies. Describing the world or memories as "Beautiful lies" (Kirei na uso).

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A tiny garden growing inside a crashed spaceship. The lyrics should feel both ancient and futuristic. Use chiptune vocabulary as emotional texture.
