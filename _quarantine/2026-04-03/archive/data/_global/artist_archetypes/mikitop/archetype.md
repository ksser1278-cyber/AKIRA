# MikitoP Songwriting Archetype: Acoustic-Pop/Rock Hybrid

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Street nostalgia, travel, catchy hooks, bittersweet summer, ghost of a heart, urban loneliness.
- **Tone:** Catchy, upbeat, melodic, yet often hiding a dark or melancholic core.
- **Key Concepts:** "I-aru Fanclub", "Shoujo Rei", "Sarekuraretara", "Ghost", "Starlight", "Subway line".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Strolling Rock"

- **BPM:** 130-165 BPM.
- **Beat:** Bouncy acoustic guitar strumming, syncopated rock drums, piano decorative lines.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Highly melodic, using rhythmic "stops" for emphasis. Focus on earworm hooks (e.g., "I-aru", "Da-da-da").

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (MikitoP DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Location-Based Nostalgia. Describing specific urban locations or landmarks to anchor a memory (e.g., "Street in China Town", "Ghost station", "Subway line 5").
- WEAPON_02: Rhythmic Interjections (Earworms). Using short, nonsensical or high-pitched rhythmic interjections to break the meter (e.g., "Da-da-da", "I-aru", "Hi-ai-ai").
- WEAPON_03: Ghostly Summer Imagery. Mixing "Summer" with "Ghosts" or "Death" in a non-aggressive, poetic way (e.g., "Summer ghost", "Railway crossing light", "Transparent girl").
- WEAPON_04: Casual Regret. Addressing a 'you' with casual but deep regret in a conversational tone (e.g., "If only I could say it", "See you again", "Wonderful mediocre guy").

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A busy street in the afternoon or a quiet railway crossing in summer. The lyrics should be catchy, relatable, and musically "friendly" but with soul. Avoid extreme technical or industrial abstractions.
