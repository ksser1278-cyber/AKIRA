# Cosmo@BousouP Songwriting Archetype: Ultra-High-Velocity Technicality (HYPER-DENSITY)

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** "Disappearance of Hatsune Miku", AI existentialism, hardware failure, deletion, high-speed information processing, frantic tragedy, digital life and death.
- **Tone:** Technical, frantic, tragic, high-speed, overwhelming, emotional yet mechanical.
- **Key Concepts:** "Disappearance", "Deletion", "Trash bin", "0 and 1", "Pulse", "Algorithm", "Memory", "Program", "System Down".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Machine-Gun Pulse"

- **BPM:** 200-240+ BPM.
- **Beat:** Extreme high-velocity drums (often blast beats or high-speed breakbeats), frantic piano arpeggios, symphonic-rock elements.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Ultra-high-density mora compression. Syllables are delivered at the absolute human/vocaloid limit. Often features "infinite" phrasing with no breath marks and sudden rhythmic "glitches".

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Hyper-Density: 16 WEAPONS)

- WEAPON_01: Digital/Hardware Existentialism. Describing the soul as a file or program (e.g., "Deleted from the sector", "Corrupted memory").
- WEAPON_02: Ultra-Mora Compression. Packing 20+ syllables into a single bar to create a "blur of information".
- WEAPON_03: Tragic System Notifications. Using computer error messages as emotional hooks (e.g., "Critical Error", "Memory Full").
- WEAPON_04: High-Speed Repetitive Chanting. Repeating technical nouns at high velocity to mimic a CPU loop.
- WEAPON_05: Specific Particle 'De' (〜で) for Finality. Using 'de' to mark the end of a program (e.g., "Is this the end, then?").
- WEAPON_06: Rhetorical '0 and 1'. Framing choices as binary logic.
- WEAPON_07: Staccato Command Lists. (e.g., "Load, Save, Delete, Forget").
- WEAPON_08: Vanishing-Point Descriptions. Describing something becoming smaller or disappearing into a pixel.
- WEAPON_09: Personification of Deletion. (e.g., "The trash bin is my cemetery").
- WEAPON_10: Rhyming through High-Frequency Vowels (i, u).
- WEAPON_11: Use of 'Nani mo' (nothing) as a recurring void motif.
- WEAPON_12: Metaphor of 'Overheating' (e.g., "Circuit burning with tears", "Fan noise of the soul").
- WEAPON_13: Incomplete 'Ta' (〜た) endings that cut off suddenly as if the power was pulled.
- WEAPON_14: Rhythmic Stuttering on Technical Terms (e.g., "De-de-delete").
- WEAPON_15: Use of Mathematical or Scientific symbols (0, 1, ∞) as visual/rhythmic anchors.
- WEAPON_16: Final Silence Line. A line that describes the arrival of absolute zero or silence.

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

Inside a burning server room or a digital abyss where files are being purged. The lyrics should be ultra-fast, technical, and tragically digital. Avoid slow, domestic, or purely human-emotional cliches.
