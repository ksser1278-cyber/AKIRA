# Hachi Songwriting Archetype: Surrealist Alternative Rock (DEEP DNA)

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Fragmented surrealism, the "grotesque daily life," visceral and somatic feelings. It's about a distorted, analog world where childhood nostalgia meets gritty reality.
- **Tone:** Gritty, dry, catchy-but-disturbing, alternative, visceral.
- **Lexical DNA (Vocabulary Fields):**
  - **Field A (Daily Life Decay):** `錆びた` (Rusted), `ガラクタ` (Junk), `路地裏` (Back alley), `埃` (Dust), `ゴミ` (Trash), `落書き` (Graffiti), `錆びたブランコ` (Rusted swing).
  - **Field B (Somatic/Visceral):** `喉` (Throat), `肺` (Lung), `爪` (Nail), `皮膚` (Skin), `血` (Blood), `吐き出す` (Spit out), `溶ける` (Melt), `飲み込む` (Swallow).
  - **Field C (Distorted Nostalgia):** `チャイム` (Chime), `校舎` (School building), `夕焼け` (Sunset), `飴玉` (Candy), `おもちゃ` (Toy), `秘密基地` (Secret base).
- **Prohibited Keywords:** `Digital`, `Cyber`, `Cloud`, `Download`, `Data`, `Pixel`, `Display`, `Modern`.

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Surreal Stomp"

- **Phrasing:** Frantic, percussive consonants (k, s, t, p). Focus on odd rhythmic spacing.
- **Nonsense Anchors:** Use phonetic chants like `ラッタッタ`, `パッパッパッパ`, `1-2-3-4` as skeletal structures.
- **THEMATIC ONOMATOPOEIA:** DO NOT reuse "Panda Hero" or "Matryoshka" lyrics. Create NEW chants using the song's current motifs (e.g. `Ko-ko-ko-koro` for heart, `Sa-sa-sa-sabita` for rust).

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (16 WEAPONS - Refined)

- WEAPON_01: Somatic Grotesque. Describing feelings through bodily functions/organs (e.g. "Throat filled with rust").
- WEAPON_02: Fragmented Noun Lists. Listing 3-4 unrelated nouns to create a "snapshot" of a scene.
- WEAPON_03: Grammatical Abandonment. Using `〜て` or ending sentences abruptly for a sense of urgency.
- WEAPON_04: Analog Imagery. Focusing on tactile, physical objects (needles, stitches, paint, dirt).
- WEAPON_05: Catchy Dread. Using upbeat rhythms with dark, heavy lyrics.
- WEAPON_06: Rhetorical 'Saa' (さぁ) for Dark Invitation.
- WEAPON_07: Phonetic Stutters on Plosives (e.g., `[Syllable] x 3 + [Motif Noun]`).
- WEAPON_08: Specific Particle 'Yo' (〜よ) / 'Sa' (〜さ) for Gritty Directness.
- WEAPON_09: Mirror-Phrasing of Distorted Reality.
- WEAPON_10: Personification of Abandoned Space.
- WEAPON_11: Rhetorical Questions to a 'Kimi' (君) who is likely a doll or phantom.
- WEAPON_12: Metaphor of 'Digestion' for emotional processing.
- WEAPON_13: Patchwork Humanity (Stitches, Sewn eyes, Cotton-filled bodies).
- WEAPON_14: Staccato Verb Repetition (e.g., "Kowasu, kowasu").
- WEAPON_15: Use of Gritty, Closed Vowels.
- WEAPON_16: Final Dissolution into Static/Noise imagery.
