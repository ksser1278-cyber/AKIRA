# VIVINOS Songwriting Archetype: Dark Fantasy Retro (Korean Core)

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Obsessive love, tragic dark fantasy, retro anime diva energy, emotional intensity.
- **Tone:** Powerful, dark, cinematic, obsessive.
- **Key Concepts:** "Alien Stage", "Ruler of My Heart", "Obsession", "Retro Diva", "Stage Tragedy".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Diva Pulse"

- **BPM:** 140-160 BPM.
- **Beat:** Orchestral rock meet 80s/90s anime synth, grand cinematic drums.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Powerful, sustained high notes, emotional vibrato, bilingual (Korean/Japanese) sensitivity.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (VIVINOS DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Obsessive Devotion Slogans. Fierce declarations of loyalty to a "Ruler" (e.g., "Only you are the owner of my heart", "My heart belongs to you").
- WEAPON_02: Dark Fantasy / Stage Imagery. Describing emotions as a performance for an alien or dark audience (e.g., "Until this stage ends", "Alien stage", "Falling rose petals").
- WEAPON_03: Cinematic Emotional Peaks. Using "Screaming" or "Singing a poem of love" as a lyrical command.
- WEAPON_04: Multilingual Core. Intersplicing Korean emotional keywords into the Japanese structure (e.g., "Simjang" (Heart), "Sarang" (Love)).

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A high-stakes singing competition on a dark, alien planet. The lyrics should feel grand, tragic, and intensely emotional. Retro-diva aesthetics.
