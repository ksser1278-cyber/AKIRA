# Teniwoha Songwriting Archetype: Identity Retro-Modern

## 1. Core Identity

- **Thematic Core:** Identity complexity, gender ambiguity, internal duality, sophisticated social commentary.
- **Tone:** Smooth, cynical, sultry, retro but modern.
- **Key Concepts:** "Villain", "Mr. / Mrs.", "Binary rejection", "Theater of self".

## 2. Rhythmic DNA: The "Androgynous Groove"

- **BPM:** 115-135 BPM.
- **Sound:** Retro-pop chord progressions, modern electronic bass, sleek synth brass.
- **Vocal Phrasing:** Smooth, syncopated, often using lower registers for an androgynous feel.

## 3. Linguistic Fingerprint Pool (Teniwoha DNA)

- WEAPON_01: Ambiguous Address (Mr. / Mrs.). Using binary titles specifically to reject them (e.g., "Sayonara Mr./Mrs.", "Neither/Both").
- WEAPON_02: Internal Anatomy Metaphors. Describing identity through strange organic/mechanical images (e.g., "Accordion heart", "Lead heart", "Internal theater").
- WEAPON_03: Sophisticated Social Spite. Refined, cold descriptions of social 'normality' (e.g., "Your correctness", "Miserable villain", "Leaning on mercy").
- WEAPON_04: Retro-Modern Pastiche. Mixing old-fashioned polite speech with harsh modern slang.

## 4. Safe Originality Zone

A jazz-age theater in a futuristic dystopian city. The lyrics should be sleek, complex, and emotionally layered. Focus on the 'mask' of identity.
